About MODI HARDWARE

Your Local Hardware Experts

MODI HARDWARE, owned and operated by Deepak Modi, has been serving the Bilaspur community for over a decade. We take pride in offering high-quality hardware products, tools, and materials for both residential and commercial projects.

Our store located at P-2, Sanjay Apartment, Vyapar Vihar Road, Bilaspur, is stocked with a comprehensive range of products from trusted brands. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you with expert advice for your hardware needs.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers through quality products, competitive pricing, and exceptional service. Whether you're a professional contractor or a DIY enthusiast, MODI HARDWARE has everything you need to get the job done right.
